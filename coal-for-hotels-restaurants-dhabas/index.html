Charcoal Supply for Hotels, Restaurants and Dhabas in Guwahati

From highway dhabas to hotel kitchens, food businesses across Guwahati run their tandoors and grills on charcoal from our Lalmati depot. What kitchens need is not just charcoal — it is charcoal that lights the same way every week, delivered before you run out.

Quick answer

Hotels, restaurants and dhabas should run tandoors and grills on natural wood charcoal (lakdi koyla) — not industrial fuel coal. Anant Coal Depot supplies restaurant-grade charcoal across all Guwahati areas with recurring weekly or fortnightly delivery, no fixed minimum order, GST billing for registered businesses, and free depot pickup at NH 37, Lalmati (open all 7 days, 8 AM–8 PM). What food businesses buy from us

The core item is natural wood charcoal — lakdi koyla — for tandoors, barbecues and grills: dry, well-carbonised lumps that build an even cooking bed. Some establishments also take small quantities of other products for non-cooking heating needs; ask and we will advise what fits.

Why kitchens stay with one depot

  • Consistency. A tandoor cook can feel when the charcoal changes. Buying from one depot with steady stock keeps the fire predictable through the year.
  • Recurring delivery. We run standing weekly or fortnightly deliveries to kitchens across Guwahati — Paltan Bazaar to Six Mile, Beltola to Jalukbari — so the tandoor never waits on an ad-hoc order. Tempo fare is borne by the customer.
  • No minimum order. A small dhaba's two bags matter as much as a hotel's bulk order. Start at whatever quantity your kitchen actually needs.
  • GST billing for registered food businesses (GSTIN 18AABHT9778F1ZI).
  • Inspection. Judge the stock yourself at the depot before switching your kitchen to it — open daily 8 AM to 8 PM.
